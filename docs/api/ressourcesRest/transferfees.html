Fees applicable to a local transfer
  • transferfees
    • v1
      • GETLIST
Service SOAP : getDomesticTransferFeeDetail
Code retour succes : 200 - OK
Uri : http://soprabanking.com/amplitude/transferfees/v1/
Description : The service getDomesticTransferFeeDetail allows viewing the fees applicable to a local transfer. The following information is required: - the nature of local transfer, - the type of local transfer, - the event linked to the local transfer, - the ordering customer account in internal format (branch, currency, number, suffix), - the amount and currency of expression of the local transfer, - the type of local transfer sending fees. The service returns the amount: - of local transfer fees, - of the commission on local transfer, - of the deduction on local transfer, - of the sending fees, - of the late penalties, - of the fees for implementation, modification and cancellation of the standing order, - of the NKS sending commissions (Cycles 1, 2 and 3), - of the NKS sending fees (Cycles 1, 2 and 3).

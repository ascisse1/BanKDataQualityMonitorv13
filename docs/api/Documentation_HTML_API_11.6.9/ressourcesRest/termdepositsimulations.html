Service SOAP : getTDSimulationDetail
Code retour succes : 200 - OK
Uri : http://soprabanking.com/amplitude/termdepositsimulations/v1/
Description : The service getTDSimulationDetail allows simulating the situation at end of term of a TD, corresponding either to a unique identifier received or to the TD detailed information received. For the selected or simulated TD, the following information is returned: - the global amount, the average amount, the amount in case of breaking for: . the gross interest, . the mandatory deductions, . the social deductions, . the social contribution, . the generalised social contribution, . the free deductions, . the taxes, . the RSA deduction (French income support), . the breaking penalties, . the net interest, - the net amount to be credited, - the net amount to be credited in case of breaking.
